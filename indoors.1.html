St Etheldreda's central

A 13th century Roman Catholic church that has largely survived intact. Escaped the worst of the Reformation by being used as the Spanish Embassy. Does a Latin Mass which is the closest you'll find to worship from the Medieval era. Somehow not a touristy place.

All Saints Margaret Street central

Just off Oxford Street, this Anglo-Catholic church is quintessential Victoriana. Also worth seeing is the Temple Church, founded by the Knights Templar in the 12th century and usually deserted.
